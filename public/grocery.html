About the Grocery Scheme

KHEN’s Grocery Scheme is designed to provide sustainable, affordable access to essential grocery items for families. Members receive structured monthly grocery support, emergency assistance, seasonal benefits, and special family bundles.

What We Offer

  • Monthly grocery support packages
  • Standard Package at the value of R150pm.
  • Exclusive Package at the value of R350.
  • Family-oriented grocery options that covers up to 10 family members.
