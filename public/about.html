Who We Are

Khen Funeral Grocery Scheme is more than a financial service — it’s a promise of dignity, unity, and care for South African families. Founded by the Sithole and Zwane families in May 2025, our mission is rooted in the spirit of Ubuntu — “I am because we are.”

Our Story

Born from a deep understanding of the struggles faced by families during times of loss, Khen Grocery Scheme was established to provide affordable grocery and catering support during funerals and family events.

We believe that no family should face financial pressure while trying to honor the memory of a loved one. Our scheme offers flexible funeral grocery plans and family coverage that bring both relief and reliability.

Our goal is simple — to help you focus on what truly matters: love, family, and remembrance. Because if they can’t, then who Khen.

Our Mission, Vision & Values

Our Vision

Community Impact

Khen Funeral Grocery Scheme continues to grow within local communities, empowering families through affordability, transparency, and care. From Dhlamini to Soweto, our name stands for reliability and Ubuntu — because we walk with our people every step of the way.
